PulseAudit

Tableau added a native Temporality setting for snapshot metrics.

Snapshot metrics — things like balances, headcount, inventory, or open cases — couldn't be defined natively in Pulse without a workaround. The workaround was an EXCLUDE LOD calculation in the measure field. It works, but it's the hard way.

If any of your metrics were built that way, PulseAudit finds them, shows you the formula, and lets you switch to the new Temporality setting in one click.
